# 01 — Project Scope  
## EventPilot AI · Physical Event Experience Companion

---

## Project Title  
**EventPilot AI** — An intelligent event companion that helps attendees navigate, plan, and connect at physical events in real time.

---

## Problem Statement  

Physical events are chaotic by design. Thousands of attendees, dozens of parallel sessions, sprawling venues, and no single source of truth — the result is a fragmented, stressful experience that leaves most people reactive rather than intentional about how they spend their time.  

Existing solutions (printed schedules, generic event apps, static maps) treat all attendees the same. They don't adapt to who you are, what you care about, or what's happening around you right now. The gap between what an event promises and what an attendee actually experiences is wide — and almost entirely a technology problem.

---

## Project Overview  

EventPilot AI is a web-based event companion powered by conversational AI and real-time data. It acts as a personal assistant for event attendees — helping them discover the right sessions, build a smart schedule, find their way around the venue, stay informed about live changes, and connect with the right people.  

The product runs in the browser, requires no app install, and is designed to work on both desktop and mobile. It integrates with event data (session catalog, venue layout, attendee profiles) to deliver a personalized, context-aware experience from the moment an attendee arrives.

---

## Core Problem Being Solved  

Attendees at physical events are overwhelmed with information, under-supported in real time, and left to navigate a complex experience entirely on their own.

### Breaking this down:

- **Discovery failure** — Attendees miss sessions that would have been valuable because they didn't know about them or couldn't filter through the noise.  
- **Schedule conflicts** — Multi-track events force hard choices with no intelligent help to reason through trade-offs.  
- **Venue friction** — Large venues are hard to navigate, especially for first-timers or attendees with accessibility needs.  
- **Live blindspots** — Room changes, cancellations, and late additions don't reach attendees fast enough or through the right channel.  
- **Networking inefficiency** — Meaningful connections happen by accident, not design.  
- **No safety net** — When something goes wrong, attendees have no clear, fast way to get help.

---

## Target Users  

| Persona | Description |
|--------|-------------|
| General Attendee | First-time or returning conference-goer who wants to make the most of their time without overwhelm |
| Speaker / Presenter | Needs quick access to their schedule, room details, and session logistics |
| Networking-focused Attendee | Prioritises meeting the right people over attending sessions |
| Attendee with Accessibility Needs | Requires accessible routes, quiet zones, and proactive support |
| Event Organiser (secondary) | Benefits indirectly through reduced support burden and better engagement data |

---

## Main Goals  

- Reduce the cognitive load of navigating a multi-track physical event.  
- Surface the right sessions, people, and moments for each attendee.  
- Keep attendees informed in real time as the event changes.  
- Make the venue feel navigable and safe.  
- Demonstrate what AI-first event tech looks like.

---

## Key Features  

### 1. Personalised Session Recommendations  
AI analyses attendee interests, professional background, and past behaviour to recommend relevant sessions.

### 2. Smart Schedule Planning  
Build personal agendas with conflict detection, buffer times, and AI-suggested alternatives.

### 3. Venue Navigation  
Interactive venue maps with walking directions, travel time, POIs, and accessibility routing.

### 4. Live Event Updates  
Real-time notifications for room changes, delays, cancellations, and announcements.

### 5. Networking Suggestions  
Suggests high-value people to meet based on shared interests and event behaviour.

### 6. Accessibility Support  
High-contrast UI, large text, screen reader support, step-free routes, quiet zones.

### 7. Help & Emergency Assistance  
Quick access to event staff, exits, first aid, and emergency procedures.

### For Organisers

### For Event Industry  
A proof-of-concept showing AI can improve real-world event experiences beyond simple chatbots.

---

## Why This Solution Matters for Physical Events  

Digital events can be managed with browser tabs and search. Physical events cannot. Real venues create location, time, and coordination challenges that require real-time intelligence.  

EventPilot AI is purpose-built for on-ground conferences, summits, expos, and professional events.
