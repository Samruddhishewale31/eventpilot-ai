# 02 — User Personas  
## EventPilot AI · Physical Event Experience Companion

---

These personas represent real behaviours, frustrations, and motivations of people attending multi-track physical events. They guide product decisions, UX flows, and AI personalisation logic.

---

# Persona 01 — The Student Attendee

## Basic Info

- **Name:** Aanya Mehta  
- **Age:** 22  
- **Role:** Final-year Computer Science Student  
- **Event Context:** First-time attendee at a 3-day tech conference on a student pass

---

## Profile

Aanya is smart, motivated, and slightly nervous. She has been excited for months, but now feels overwhelmed by the huge venue, multiple tracks, and dozens of sessions.

She wants to learn, make new connections, and feel confident.

---

## Goals

- Find sessions related to ML and system design  
- Meet students or early-career attendees  
- Attend the two keynote sessions  
- Navigate the venue smoothly  

---

## Pain Points

- Too many sessions, hard to choose  
- Doesn’t know which speakers matter most  
- Networking feels intimidating  
- Missed orientation due to long registration queue  

---

## How EventPilot AI Helps

- AI recommends the best sessions based on her interests  
- Creates a schedule in minutes  
- Sends targeted room-change alerts  
- Provides navigation directions  
- Suggests nearby attendees with shared interests  

---

## Critical Features

- Session Recommendations  
- Schedule Builder  
- Venue Navigation  
- Live Updates  
- Networking Suggestions

# Persona 02 — The Professional Attendee

## Basic Info

- **Name:** Rohan Desai  
- **Age:** 34  
- **Role:** Senior Product Manager  
- **Event Context:** Attending a 2-day industry summit sponsored by his company

---

## Profile

Rohan is efficient, experienced, and focused on ROI. He knows the real value of events comes from networking and high-value conversations.

He quickly judges whether an app is worth using.

---

## Goals

- Meet three specific people  
- Attend two important sessions  
- Monitor all tracks efficiently  
- Leave with actionable follow-ups  

---

## Pain Points

- Networking is noisy and random  
- No time to read long session descriptions  
- Session clashes with networking moments  
- Existing apps feel static and outdated  

---

## How EventPilot AI Helps

- Finds target contacts attending the event  
- Suggests ideal meeting windows and locations  
- Resolves schedule conflicts intelligently  
- Highlights only relevant sessions  

---

## Critical Features

- Networking Suggestions  
- Smart Schedule Builder  
- Live Updates  
- Session Recommendations  

---

# Persona 03 — The Organiser / Support Staff

## Basic Info

- **Name:** Priya Nair  
- **Age:** 29  
- **Role:** Event Operations Coordinator  
- **Event Context:** Managing live logistics for a large conference

---

## Profile

Priya handles everything when things go wrong—room changes, delays, attendee confusion, staff coordination.

She wants attendees to have a smooth experience but is overloaded.

---

## Goals

- Reduce repetitive support queries  
- Push urgent updates quickly  
- Support attendees with accessibility needs  
- Keep safety information available  

---

## Pain Points

- Attendees ignore emails and notices  
- Room changes cause confusion instantly  
- Staff waste time giving directions  
- No live view of attendee problems  

---

## How EventPilot AI Helps

- Sends targeted alerts only to affected attendees  
- Provides self-service help center  
- Routes accessibility requests correctly  
- Reduces info desk pressure  

---

## Critical Features

- Live Updates  
- Help & Emergency Assistance  
- Accessibility Support  
- Venue Navigation  

---

# Persona 04 — Accessibility-Focused Attendee

## Basic Info

- **Name:** Vikram Iyer  
- **Age:** 41  
- **Role:** UX Researcher  
- **Event Context:** Wheelchair user attending a design conference

---

## Profile

Vikram is experienced but expects friction. He needs step-free routes, accessible rooms, and reliable real-time updates.

He wants equal experience, not special treatment.

---

## Goals

- Know which rooms are accessible  
- Get alerts with accessibility context  
- Find restrooms, lifts, quiet zones easily  
- Use one inclusive platform  

---

## Pain Points

- Accessibility info is hidden or missing  
- Room changes create last-minute stress  
- Maps ignore accessible routing  
- Must repeatedly explain needs  

---

## How EventPilot AI Helps

- Accessibility Mode customises the whole UI  
- Navigation defaults to step-free routes  
- Alerts mention accessibility status of new rooms  
- Map highlights lifts, entrances, restrooms, quiet rooms  
- Dedicated support contact available  

---

## Critical Features

- Accessibility Support  
- Venue Navigation  
- Live Updates  
- Help Assistance  
- Schedule Builder
